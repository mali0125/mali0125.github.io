About Me

My name is Zarmeen Malik. I recently graduated from the University of Ottawa with an Honours BSc in Biomedical Science (French Immersion) and Minor in Psychology. Currently, I am enrolled in the Interactive Media Design Program at Algonquin College. I am interested in learning more about the IT field to expand my knowledge and skills. I would also like to learn more about design, and take this opportunity to express creativity through my work.

"Without knowledge action is useless and knowledge without action is futile" - Abu Bakr

There are various activities that I enjoy during my free time that also reflect some of my interests. For instance, I like visual arts (sketching, colouring, and painting), playing video games, learning Japanese, and reading comics/manga. From my perspective, it is important to discover positive activities and interests in order to become a more knowledgeable individual.
