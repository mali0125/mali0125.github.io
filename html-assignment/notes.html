Text Semantics

Emphasis
The emphasis tag will italicize text to show importance (ex. Sample words)
Strong
The strong tag will bolden text to show more importance (ex. Sample words)
Mark
The mark tag will highlight text (ex. Sample words)
Sub
The sub tag will display text as a subscript (ex. y1)
Sup
The sup tag will display text as a superscript (ex. x2)
